FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before committing.

How quickly will we see results? +

Most clients see their first qualified meetings booked within 72 hours of campaign launch. A full pipeline rhythm typically establishes within the first 2–3 weeks as we optimise messaging based on early response data.

Is there a minimum contract length? +

Monthly plans can be cancelled after the initial 30-day onboarding period — no long-term lock-in. Annual plans are billed quarterly and offer 20% savings over the monthly rate.

How is this different from hiring an SDR? +

A full-time SDR costs £35–50k+ per year before tools, management time, and ramp-up. OutreachOS delivers a complete outreach system — LinkedIn, email, AI voice, data intelligence, and reporting — at a fraction of that cost, active from day one.

What does a "qualified meeting" actually mean? +

A qualified meeting is a confirmed calendar booking with a decision-maker who has passed our AI voice qualification layer — confirming company fit, budget awareness, and genuine interest before we hand the meeting to you.

What industries do you work with? +

We work with B2B service businesses across 18+ sectors including professional services, financial advisory, SaaS, recruitment, consultancy, and managed services. If you sell a high-ticket B2B service, we can build your pipeline.

Is the data GDPR compliant? +

Yes, fully. Every data source we use — LinkedIn, Companies House, and our proprietary verification layer — is processed in accordance with UK GDPR. Legitimate interest is documented for every outreach campaign, and opt-out is handled automatically.

Can you integrate with our CRM? +

Growth and Enterprise plans include native sync with HubSpot and Salesforce. We also support Zapier-based integrations for other CRMs. All contact data, outreach history, and meeting notes are pushed automatically on booking.
